Build for the customer journeys of tomorrow. To stay ahead of your competitors and keep winning in the next era of digital commerce, B2B companies need to collaborate with suppliers of commerce technology that help then adapt to fast changing marktets. Together with Henk Mensinga from Youwe I discussed how a composable commerce approach helps B2B companies to form a foundation for their futureproof e-commerce strategy.
